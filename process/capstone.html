    Mentor Feedback : Wayne Seltzer

    Think about what makes this different, WHY are you making this
    If you care about aesthetics, then you should lean into to the live performance aspect of the product Digital or analog ?
    Narrow scope, creating effects is much more difficult than you might think
    Pitch correction is probably going to be extremely difficult and not worth pursuing

    Think about what code you want to put onto your seed, this should be based on the specific vocal effects you want and what program makes them the easiest to code

    Week Six Assignment (02/20)

    Ana Mahadevan


    There comes a point in a project where you realize the way that you've stubbornly been going down is wrong,
    and that you won't be able to complete things unless you pivot. And, finally accept that you have to let go of
    your preconceptions and try something different.

    That's where I hit earlier this week, after a long difficult conversation with one of my mentors.
    Now, i'm moving on to using a slightly different interface with a different chip that should at the very least
    allow me to easily prototype the actual software which controls the function of what i'm trying to do without having to worry about faulty wiring
    Now i've been focusing on solely software , the daisy pod is actually running audio which feels like a huge step but ultimately one that I should have made weeks earlier.
    I'm trying to keep myself accountable now, by setting more specific goals for days and overall weeks.
