    Week Four Assignment (02/06)

    Week three progress (on UX Testing):

    Usability interviews :

    Filip Casey (Lead singer of telecine)
    Initial thoughts without seeing product:
    Said he would personally look for a delay feature first, he wants to be able to change delay and decay time.
    - bit crush
    - hard clipping distortion
    - no reverb , because room is non treated
    - freeze

    Zade Baldwin ( Vocalist , guitar player, my drummer )
    Initial thoughts without seeing product:
    Wants digital effects that wouldn't be available from basic analog pedals he could pre-amp
    - pitch correction
    - reverb
    - delay
    - distortion

    User scenarios/use cases/personas:

    Artists within different genre performances, shifts in music with punk vs shoegaze vs indie artists
