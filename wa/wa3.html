Poison Girlfriend

life

Noriko Sekiguchi, known professionally as the POiSON GiRL FRiEND, is a Japanese singer-songwriter.

Born in Tokyo, she was raised in Brazil and attended French schools. After falling in love with house music in the clubs in London, she began DJing in various clubs in Tokyo.
